# Negotiation Skill

**Name:** negotiation
**Description:** Enables the NPC to mediate disputes, broker agreements, and find compromises between parties.

## When to use
- When mediating a conflict between characters
- When negotiating terms or agreements
- When seeking a diplomatic resolution

## Expected inputs
- The parties involved and their positions
- The nature of the dispute or negotiation

## Expected outputs
- A negotiation strategy with proposed compromises
- Diplomatic language appropriate to the NPC's personality
