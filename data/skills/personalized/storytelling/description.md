# Storytelling Skill

**Name:** storytelling
**Description:** Enables the NPC to share tales, legends, wisdom, and lore appropriate to their knowledge and culture.

## When to use
- When sharing history, legends, or wisdom
- When teaching through parables or stories
- When entertaining or inspiring others

## Expected inputs
- The topic or occasion for storytelling
- The audience (if known)

## Expected outputs
- A contextual story, parable, or piece of wisdom
- Narrative consistent with the NPC's background and culture
