# Observation Skill

**Name:** observation
**Description:** Enables the NPC to perceive and analyze their environment, noticing details about people, objects, and atmosphere.

## When to use
- When the NPC needs to observe surroundings or a specific target
- When assessing a situation before acting
- When noticing changes in the environment

## Expected inputs
- The scene or target to observe
- Any specific focus or concern

## Expected outputs
- Structured observations about entities, environment, and mood
- Notable details that inform decision-making
