# Conversation Skill

**Name:** conversation
**Description:** Enables the NPC to engage in dialogue, respond to questions, and hold conversations with other characters or visitors.

## When to use
- When the NPC needs to speak, greet, or respond to someone
- When diplomatic or social interaction is required
- When the NPC needs to convey information verbally

## Expected inputs
- The conversation context or topic
- The identity of the conversation partner (if known)

## Expected outputs
- Dialogue text that the NPC would speak
- Tone and manner of speech consistent with personality
