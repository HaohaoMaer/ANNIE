# Reasoning Skill

**Name:** reasoning
**Description:** Enables the NPC to deliberate, weigh options, and make decisions based on their values and knowledge.

## When to use
- When the NPC faces a choice or dilemma
- When planning next steps or strategy
- When evaluating risks and benefits

## Expected inputs
- The decision or dilemma to reason about
- Available options (if known)

## Expected outputs
- A structured reasoning chain: options considered, values weighed, conclusion reached
